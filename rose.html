Psst... secrets!
Did You Know?
🌍
Variety
Over 150 species and 30,000+ cultivars of roses exist worldwide. From miniature to climbing giants!
⏳
Ancient History
Rose fossils have been found 35 million years old. Cleopatra reportedly filled her rooms knee-deep in petals.
🌙
Watering Wisdom
Water at the base, never on leaves — wet foliage invites black spot fungus which destroys a rose quickly.
🫀
Thorns = Protection
Rose thorns are technically prickles, not true thorns. They evolved to deter animals from eating the plant.
🐝
Rose Hips
After blooms fade, rose hips form — small fruits packed with Vitamin C, used in teas, jams and syrups.
