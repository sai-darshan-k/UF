My Identity Card
Anthurium / Flamingo Flower
Anthurium andraeanum
Wax Queen
Tropical Perennial Colombian Origin Evergreen Waxy Spathe 6 Vivid Colours

Anthurium is the showstopper of the plant world — its heart-shaped waxy spathe flowers look so perfect they are often mistaken for plastic. Originally from the rainforests of Colombia and Ecuador, it thrives indoors in bright indirect light. The flowers are not actually petals but a modified leaf called a spathe, surrounding the true flower spike called a spadix. Each bloom lasts 6 to 8 weeks, making it one of the longest-lasting flowering houseplants in existence. Available in six spectacular colours.

The Flower That Lasts Two Months
Unlike most flowers that fade in days, a single Anthurium bloom stays vibrant and waxy for 6 to 8 weeks. With a healthy plant producing 4 to 6 blooms at a time, you essentially have a permanently flowering indoor display all year long.

Psst… secrets!
Did You Know?
Not Actually a Flower
The colourful heart-shaped part of an Anthurium is NOT a flower — it is a modified leaf called a spathe. The true flower is the tiny spike (spadix) rising from the centre. That is why it lasts so much longer than regular flowers.
1000+ Wild Species
Anthurium is one of the largest genera in the plant kingdom with over 1,000 wild species discovered across tropical America. The cultivated Anthurium andraeanum you keep at home is just one of this enormous family.
Toxic — Handle With Care
All parts of Anthurium contain calcium oxalate crystals which cause intense burning and swelling if chewed or ingested. Keep away from pets and children. Wearing gloves when pruning is recommended for sensitive skin.
Attracts Pollinators Cleverly
In the wild, Anthurium releases a subtle sweet scent from its spadix to attract beetles and flies as pollinators. The shiny spathe acts as a landing reflector, guiding insects directly to the pollen.
Hawaii's Export Champion
Anthurium is Hawaii's number one cut flower export, generating millions in revenue annually. The blooms are so long-lasting that they are shipped worldwide and still look fresh on arrival weeks later.
