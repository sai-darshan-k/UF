Psst… secrets!
Did You Know?
🏺
Ancient Medicine
Aloe Vera has been used medicinally for over 6,000 years ancient Egyptians called it the "Plant of Immortality" and used it in burial rites.
💧
Water Storage Champion
Aloe leaves are 95% water stored in a thick gel this is why it survives months of drought and why the gel is so hydrating on skin.
🧬
75 Active Compounds
The gel contains 75 active compounds vitamins A, C, E, B12, folic acid, enzymes, minerals, salicylic acids, and amino acids. No lab can replicate it!
🌿
NASA Approved
Featured in NASA's Clean Air Study as one of the best plants for purifying formaldehyde from indoor air most common in new furniture and paint.
⚠️
Latex Warning
The yellow latex layer between skin and gel is a laxative avoid consuming it. Only the clear inner gel is safe for food and skin use.
