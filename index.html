The Process

How Urban Farmer
Works

Whether you run a nursery or love plants, every step of the journey is intelligently covered.

Register and Get Verified
Create your nursery profile. Our team verifies and fully onboards you within 24 hours with QR setup and dashboard access.
List Your Plants
Upload plants yourself or request an Urban Farmer executive to visit, photograph, and professionally list your entire collection.
Deploy QR Codes
Each plant cluster gets a physical QR. Customers scan, learn, and purchase directly. Orders flow to your dashboard automatically.
Grow with Analytics
Track scans, sales, top plants, and revenue in real-time. Expand via marketplace listings, corporate tie-ups, and sensor services.
Know Before You Buy
Scan a plant's QR code at any partner nursery. Get videos, care guides, botanical facts, and uses before making any decision.
Buy Online or Rent
Purchase online for delivery or pick up in-store. Or explore our rent-a-plant program for events, offices, and seasonal homes.
Download the App
Get the Urban Farmer app for AI plant doctor, sensor monitoring, gamified care reminders, and booking gardeners on demand.
Make Your Home Green
From balcony gardens to terrace farms — book gardeners, install sensors, and join a community that grows together.
