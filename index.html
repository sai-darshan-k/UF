How Urban Farmer Works

Whether you run a nursery or love plants, every step of the journey is covered - intelligently.

01
Register and Get Verified
Create your nursery profile with business details, location, and contact. Our team verifies your listing within 24 hours and onboards you fully.
02
List Your Plants
Upload plant names and photos yourself, or request an Urban Farmer executive to visit, photograph, and list your entire collection professionally.
03
Deploy QR Codes In-Nursery
Each plant cluster gets a physical QR. Visitors scan to learn about plants and purchase directly. Orders flow to your analytics dashboard automatically.
04
Grow with Smart Analytics
Track sales, scans, top plants, and revenue. Advanced sensors, marketplace listings, corporate tie-ups, and event supply expand your reach further.
